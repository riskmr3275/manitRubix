Joining the Rubik Club isn't just about solving puzzles, it's about embracing a world of endless possibilities and cultural enrichment

The Rubik Club serves as a welcoming haven for enthusiasts of all skill levels, from beginners tentatively twisting their first layers to seasoned solvers mastering advanced techniques. Through workshops, tutorials, and friendly competitions, we empower our members to sharpen their problem-solving skills, cultivate patience, and embrace the joy of discovery. Beyond the physical manipulation of the Cube, our club fosters a supportive community where individuals can share strategies, exchange tips, and celebrate each other's successes. .

NIT Bhopal

The National Institute of Technology, Bhopal, commonly referred to as NIT Bhopal or MANIT (Maulana Azad National Institute of Technology), is one of the premier engineering institutes in India. Established in 1960 as the Maulana Azad College of Technology (MACT), it was later upgraded to the status of NIT in 2002 and renamed MANIT. NIT Bhopal offers undergraduate, postgraduate, and doctoral programs in various disciplines of engineering, technology, architecture, and science. The institute has state-of-the-art facilities, experienced faculty, and a strong focus on research and innovation. It is located in Bhopal, the capital city of Madhya Pradesh, India. NIT Bhopal has consistently been ranked among the top engineering institutes in India and is renowned for its academic excellence and contributions to the field of engineering and technology.
